Understanding Street-Legal EVolution MSVs

What is an EVolution Medium Speed Vehicle (EVolution MSV)?

An EVolution Medium Speed Vehicle (EVolution MSV) is a federally approved class of four-wheeled vehicles that have a maximum speed of 20-25 mph and a gross vehicle weight rating of less than 3,000 pounds. EVolution MSVs are street legal on roads with posted speed limits of 35 mph or less in most states, making them perfect for neighborhood transportation, planned communities, college campuses, and small towns.

EVolution MSVs are equipped with required safety features including headlights, turn signals, mirrors, windshield, seat belts, and more. These vehicles provide convenient transportation with lower operating costs than traditional vehicles.

EVolution MSV Regulations and Requirements

While federal regulations define the basic requirements for EVolution MSVs, specific laws vary by state and local jurisdiction. Generally, EVolution MSVs must include:

  • Headlights, taillights, and brake lights
  • Turn signals and hazard lights
  • Mirrors (rearview and side)
  • Windshield
  • Seat belts for all seating positions
  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • Parking brake
  • Speedometer
  • Reflectors

Most states require EVolution MSV registration, insurance, and a valid driver's license to operate on public roads. Always check your local regulations before purchasing an EVolution MSV.
