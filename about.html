About EVolution Medium Speed Vehicles

The Future of Neighborhood Transportation

What Are EVolution Medium Speed Vehicles?

EVolution Medium Speed Vehicles (EVolution MSVs) are innovative electric vehicles designed for efficient, eco-friendly transportation in neighborhoods, planned communities, campuses, and small towns. These vehicles combine the convenience of a golf cart with the safety features and functionality of a traditional automobile, creating the perfect solution for short-distance travel.

EVolution MSVs are street-legal on roads with posted speed limits of 35 mph or less in most states, making them ideal for daily commuting, running errands, and recreational use within your community. With zero emissions, low operating costs, and minimal maintenance requirements, EVolution MSVs represent a sustainable alternative to conventional vehicles for many transportation needs.

Key Features of EVolution Medium Speed Vehicles

  • Street Legal: Equipped with all required safety features including headlights, turn signals, mirrors, windshield, seat belts, and more
  • Electric Powered: Zero emissions with advanced battery technology for extended range
  • Speed Range: Typically 20-25 mph maximum speed, perfect for neighborhood travel
  • Versatile: Available in various configurations for passenger transport, utility use, or specialized applications
  • Cost-Effective: Lower purchase price than conventional vehicles with minimal operating costs
  • Low Maintenance: Fewer moving parts than gas vehicles means less maintenance and longer service life
  • Customizable: Wide range of options and accessories to personalize your EVolution MSV
