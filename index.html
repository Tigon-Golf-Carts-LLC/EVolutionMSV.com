Key Characteristics of a Medium Speed Golf Cart (MSV)

  • Speed Range: Typically reaches 30 to 45 mph, depending on state regulations.
  • Street Legal: Allowed on public roads with speed limits up to 45 mph.
  • Required Equipment: Includes seat belts, mirrors, headlights, tail lights, turn signals, windshields, and DOT-compliant safety features.
  • Best For: Neighborhood driving, campus transport, resorts, municipal use, and light commercial duties.

MSV Golf Cart vs. LSV Golf Cart

  • LSV Golf Carts: Max speed of 25 mph. Legal on roads posted at 35 mph or lower.
  • MSV Golf Carts: Like EVolution MSVs, they reach up to 45 mph and are legal on higher-speed roads, depending on state laws.
